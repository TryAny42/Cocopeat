About Us

Our Story

We are a company that operate as both a construction and export company specializing in high-quality materials for interior design and cocopeat products. Through our partnerships with Indonesian MSMEs (Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises), we ensure premium quality standards while supporting local businesses and bringing their exceptional products to global markets.

Why Choose Us?

  • Deliver exceptional results by balancing functionality, comfort, and aesthetics
  • Professionals design team with experience
  • Reliable shipping and logistics network
  • Customized solutions for every client
  • Competitive pricing with excellent customer service
